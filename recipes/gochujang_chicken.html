Gochujang Chicken

Description

Sweet and sour gochujang chicken is a quick wok dish featuring Korean flavors. Serve over rice.

Ingredients

  • Gochujang
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Honey
  • Garlic
  • Sesame Oil
  • Ginger
  • Canola oil
  • Chicken
  • Garnishes
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Make sauce in bowl. Cook chicken in wok.
  2. Add sauce to the chicken in the wok. Cook until thick and bubbling.
  3. Garnish and serve over rice.
